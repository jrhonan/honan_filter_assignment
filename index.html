Where To Stream 2021's Oscar Predictions

Written by John Honan | Published on March 2, 2021

After a rough year for cinema, the 93 Annual Academy Awards will take place on April 25th. Nominations will not be announced for two weeks; however, the following eight films are eligible for nomination.

All eight of the predicted best picture nominees are available for streaming from the comfort of your own home. New Yorkers ready to get back to the theater are also able to see all of the predicted nominees, except for Netflix exclusives The Trial of the Chicago Seven and Mank. On Friday March 5, NYC movie theaters opened their doors at 25% capacity - capped at 50 people - to movie buffs eager to support their favorite theaters.

Box office revenue from this past weekend is an estimated $25 million, as reported by the Hollywood Reporter.
